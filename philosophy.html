My Coding Philosophy



Just starting out in the coding world, but my goals are to create beautiful, well-factored code that can be changed easily and improved upon by collaborators. My philosophy stems from a place of empathy for user experience. Technology is supposed to enhance lives, not frustrate it. To that end, I like to create products that do not assume previous technical knowledge.
  • To create products borne out of empathy
  • To always produce quality code
  • To help marginalized communities lead the way
Many communities, marginalized by race, gender and ability, are not able to use tech to the fullest extent. That needs to end. Never sacrifice quality for speed. Keep our communities working together and innovating for tomorrow.
